all interoperating through tiger style principles and modern monetary theory

## the lens: tiger style + MMT

### tiger style principles

**explicit control:**
- no hidden complexity
- explicit memory management
- explicit error handling
- explicit dependencies

**minimalism:**
- simplicity through subtraction
- minimal dependencies
- static allocation where possible
- zero-copy techniques

**intentionality:**
- every design decision is explicit
- every abstraction is intentional
- every dependency is justified

### modern monetary theory (MMT)

**sovereign currency:**
- currency is a public utility
- sovereign currency issuers are not revenue-constrained
- spending creates money, taxation destroys it
- focus on real resource constraints, not financial constraints

**functional finance:**
- fiscal policy should target full employment and price stability
- monetary policy supports fiscal policy
- public purpose over private profit

**economic democracy:**
- democratic control over money creation
- public investment in public goods
- job guarantee programs
- green new deal principles

**key thinkers:**
- Stephanie Kelton: "The Deficit Myth"
- L. Randall Wray: MMT foundations
- focus on real resources, not financial constraints
